ComInfo

ComInfo, the focal point of this project, is designed to facilitate computing students in acquiring essential information about key entities within President University. These include the President University Faculty Association (PUFA) of Computing, President University Major Association (PUMA) of Informatics, President University Major Association (PUMA) of Information Systems, and President University Major Association (PUMA) of Visual Communication Design. ComInfo helps students get info about the organization in one place. It's not just about facts but also about connecting students with their teachers and ensuring they know about events happening in their academic community. The login/register feature is there to make it more personalized for each student. Cominfo is a handy tool that brings students closer to their academic community at President University.
